- **Call to Action:** Add clear next steps: "Shop Sustainable Solutions" or "Discover Your Green Office Upgrade"

**Overall:** The ad has solid foundation but needs more authentic, practical visual storytelling and empowering copy that speaks to small business owners' dual priorities of efficiency and sustainability.
