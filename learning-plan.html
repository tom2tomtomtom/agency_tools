Refining AI Output: Tips for Better Results

AI interaction is often an iterative process. If the initial output isn't exactly what you need, here are some strategies to refine the AI's response:

  • Be More Specific: If the output is too general, provide more precise instructions. For example, instead of "Summarize this meeting," try "Summarize this meeting in 5 bullet points, focusing on action items and decisions."
  • Adjust Tone and Style: If the tone is off, explicitly state the desired tone in your prompt (e.g., "Adopt a formal tone," "Be concise and direct"). You can also provide examples of the preferred style in your uploaded knowledge.
  • Provide More Context: If the AI misses key information, it might be lacking sufficient context. Upload more relevant knowledge files or include additional background details directly in your prompt.
  • Break Down Complex Tasks: For very complex requests, break them into smaller, sequential steps. Guide the AI through each step, building on previous outputs.
  • Ask Clarifying Questions: If the AI seems to misunderstand, ask it to clarify its interpretation or to rephrase its understanding of your request.
  • Iterate with Feedback: Provide specific feedback on what needs to change. For example, "This summary is good, but please expand on the budget discussion and include who is responsible for each task."
  • Experiment with Keywords: Try different keywords or phrases in your prompt to see if they elicit a more accurate response.

Troubleshooting & Support Framework

Self-Service Troubleshooting Guide

GREEN ZONE - Try First: Common Issues You Can Solve

Problem: "AI output isn't specific enough to our work"

Solution:

  • Upload more specific knowledge files (client briefs, past work examples)
  • Add client name and project details to your prompt
  • Reference specific Monigle methodologies in your request

Problem: "Output quality varies significantly"

Solution:

  • Check knowledge file quality - upload your best examples
  • Be more specific in your requests (ask for exact format, length, style)
  • Test with different knowledge combinations

Problem: "Claude/ChatGPT isn't understanding our industry context"

Solution:

  • Include more healthcare/financial services examples in uploads
  • Add regulatory context to prompts when relevant
  • Reference specific compliance requirements in requests
YELLOW ZONE - Ask Your Ambassador: Issues for Ambassador Support
  • Setting up team Custom GPTs for sharing
  • Integrating multiple solutions into workflows
  • Customizing prompts for specific client types
  • Creating team knowledge sharing systems
RED ZONE - Book with Tom Hyde: Issues That Need Expert Support
  • Technical setup problems that ambassadors can't solve
  • Client confidentiality concerns with AI tools
  • Strategic questions about AI implementation
  • Advanced customization and integration needs
